Size Guide

All measurements in centimeters unless stated. Garments are measured flat. Expect ±1.5cm production tolerance.

Instructions

How to Measure Your Garment

All sizes refer to garment measurements (not body measurements). For the best fit, compare against a garment you already own.

📏

Chest Width

Measure flat across the front, 1 inch below the armhole seam. Multiply by 2 for the full circumference.

📐

Body Length

From the highest point of the shoulder at the neck seam, straight down to the bottom hem.

✂️

Sleeve Length

From the top of the shoulder seam to the end of the sleeve opening, measured flat along the top of the sleeve.

🧵

Shoulder Width

From the left shoulder seam to the right shoulder seam, measured straight across the back, flat.

Fit Reference

Fit Type Guide

Understanding how our different series are designed to fit.

👔

Regular / Essential

Classic straight silhouette. True-to-size. Chest with some ease. Suitable for most body types and print-ready applications.

SH-101 / SH-102 / SH-403
🧥

Boxy / Heavyweight

Wider chest, shorter length relative to width. Relaxed through the body. Runs one size bigger than essential — size down for a standard fit.

SH-204 / SH-205 / SH-206
🫧

Oversized / Drop-Shoulder

Intentionally oversized with extended shoulder seam drop. Designed to be worn as a fashion statement. Size down 1–2 sizes for a more standard look.

SH-305 / SH-306 / SH-307
