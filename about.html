Our Story

Manufactured in China.

Senhong has been producing world-class blank T-shirts since 2010. From a single knitting machine to a 12,000 sqm facility — our story is built on craftsmanship and trust.

Who We Are

More Than a Factory.

Senhong Apparel was built with one question in mind: "Why is it so hard to find a truly great blank T-shirt at wholesale prices?"

We answer that question every day by obsessing over yarn selection, knitting tension, dye consistency, and stitch quality. Every SKU in our catalog has been stress-tested by our in-house QC lab before it reaches your hands.

Today, we supply over 150 brands and distributors across North America, Europe, Australia, and Southeast Asia — from independent streetwear labels to Fortune 500 corporate gifting programs.

Our Journey

14 Years of Growth.

2010 — Founded
Starting from Scratch
Senhong began in Guangzhou with 3 circular knitting machines and 12 workers, producing basic cotton tees for local markets.
2013 — First Export
Going International
Secured our first US client — a 10,000 pc wholesale order for a California-based print shop. The beginning of our global journey.
2016 — ISO Certified
Quality System Formalized
Achieved ISO 9001:2015 and OEKO-TEX® Standard 100 certification. Invested in German dyeing equipment for color consistency.
2019 — New Factory
12,000 sqm Campus Opens
Moved to our purpose-built 12,000 sqm facility with in-house lab testing, embroidery department, and print-ready finishing line.
2024 — Today
150+ Global Partners
Supplying 150+ brands across 40 countries with 40+ SKUs across 4 fabric series. Expanding organic and sustainable cotton lines.
