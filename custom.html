What We Offer

OEM or ODM — What's the Difference?

🏭

OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer)

You bring your own design, tech pack, or sample. We manufacture exactly to your specifications — fabric, construction, labels, and packaging are all based on your requirements.

  • You control the design 100%
  • Ideal for established brands with spec sheets
  • MOQ from 500 pcs per style/color
💡

ODM (Original Design Manufacturer)

Start from scratch. Our in-house design team works with you to develop a product concept — choosing fabric, fit, construction, and branding elements from our existing library.

  • No design experience needed
  • We provide mood boards, spec sheets, 3D mockups
  • MOQ from 1,000 pcs per new development
How It Works

From Idea to Your Hands — 5 Steps

💬
Step 01
Consultation
Share your vision — mood boards, references, sketches, or a tech pack. We'll review within 48 hours.
📐
Step 02
Development
Our pattern makers develop the garment spec and send you a costed quote with tech pack draft.
👕
Step 03
Sampling
We produce 1–3 physical samples. You review, approve, or request revisions. Up to 2 revisions included.
🏭
Step 04
Production
30% deposit triggers production. We send weekly updates with photos from the factory floor.
🚀
Step 05
QC & Ship
AQL 2.5 final inspection. Ship via DHL / sea freight. Full shipping docs and compliance certs included.
What Can Be Customized

Customize Every Detail

Everything on the garment can be made to your exact specification.

🧵

Fabric & Weight

Choose GSM (150–400), yarn count, knit structure — single jersey, french terry, waffle, etc.

🎨

Custom Color

Match any Pantone TPX/TPG reference. Custom dyeing available from 500 pcs per color.

📐

Fit & Pattern

Regular, slim, boxy, oversized, drop-shoulder. Custom grading and women's / children's sizing.

🏷️

Neck Label

Woven label, printed label, heat transfer label, or tear-away tag. Your brand, your call.

🔖

Hang Tags & Packaging

Custom hang tags (printed, embossed, die-cut), poly bags with header cards, tissue wrap.

🪡

Construction Details

Sleeve length, shoulder drop, seam type (flatlock / overlock), cuff style, rib collar size.

🖨️

Printing & Decoration

Screen print, DTG, embroidery, puff print, heat transfer — applied before or after cutting.

🫧

Finishing

Garment wash (acid, enzyme, pigment), stone wash, distressed effects, suede finish.

📄

Care Labels

Multi-language care labels for US, EU, UK compliance. Fiber content labeling by country.
